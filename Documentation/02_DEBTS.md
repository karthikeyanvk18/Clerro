## Tab Views

### **All Debts Tab** (Default)
- Shows all active and inactive debts
- Includes both loans and credit cards
- Total count displayed

### **Active Tab**
- Only currently active debts
- Sorted by due date
- Count of active debts

### **Paid Off Tab**
- Closed/completed debts
- Shows payoff date
- Historical reference

### **Defaulted Tab** (if applicable)
- Overdue or missed payments
- Requires action
- Highest priority
